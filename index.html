FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

How long does a project take?

Timelines depend on the project scope. A logo can be done in 1–2 days, a full website in 5–10 days, and a complete brand identity in 1–2 weeks. I always give you a clear timeline before we begin.

Do you work with AI tools?

Yes! DRT-VERSE uses AI-powered tools to enhance speed and quality without losing the human creative touch. This means faster delivery, better results, and more value for your money.

How many revisions do I get?

Every project includes at least 2–3 rounds of revisions. I work until you're 100% happy with the result. Additional revisions beyond the scope can be arranged at a small fee.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll get all standard formats — PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, Figma source files, and more — depending on the service. Everything you need to use your design immediately.

How do I pay?

Payment is accepted via UPI, bank transfer, or other digital methods. Typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For product purchases, full payment is required at checkout.
